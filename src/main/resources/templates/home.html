Используемые типы операций
addition - сложение
subtraction - вычитание
multiplication - умножение
division - деление

Пример GET запроса

http://localhost:8080/calculate?first_operand=10&second_operand=3&type_operation=addition
http://localhost:8080/calculate?first_operand=10&second_operand=3&type_operation=subtraction
http://localhost:8080/calculate?first_operand=10&second_operand=3&type_operation=multiplication
http://localhost:8080/calculate?first_operand=10&second_operand=3&type_operation=division
